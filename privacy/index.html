Don Naipe Privacy Policy

(last updated 16th November 2018)

Don Naipe respects your privacy. We are committed to protect your personal information. Don Naipe believes that users have the right to know which are the practices followed in their games regarding their personal information.

GDPR

The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) is designed to strengthen rights to privacy and is effective from 25th May 2018.

This Privacy Policy shows what information is collected and what it is used for.

Anonymous data

Don Naipe may collect and use anonymous data through Google Analytics. We cannot identify you from this data that is only employed to get usage data of our games such as general statistics on our audience. This helps us to understand our players and to determine how to update our games in order to better support them.

Emailed personal information

Any personal information sent to us by email will be stored by our email systems. Your email address will be used to respond to your enquiries.

We will not ask for any personal information that we do not need.

If you are subscribed to our newsletter, we will send news about Don Naipe a few times a year. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any of our newsletters.

Ad networks

Our mobile games are supported by advertisements that are provided by third-party ad networks. These ad networks may employ identifiers assigned to your device that do not allow you to be identified as a person. These identifiers are used to provide personalized ads.

You can opt in or out of personalized ads at any time. If you opt out, you will still get ads in our games, but they will not be personalized for you.

Opting out of personalized ads in Android devices:
Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of ads personalization

Opting out of personalized ads in iOS devices:
Settings > Privacy > Ads > Limit ad tracking

Don Naipe decides to only provide ad networks with a minimal set of permissions in our games. We only use ad networks compliant with their GDPR obligations. The privacy policies of these ad networks are listed below:

https://policies.google.com/privacy?hl=en
https://www.applovin.com/privacy/
https://answers.chartboost.com/en-us/articles/200780269
https://www.facebook.com/policy.php
https://www.inmobi.com/privacy-policy/
http://www.mopub.com/legal/privacy/
http://vungle.com/privacy/

Please be aware that any information provided as a result of your interaction with these ads is not governed by our Privacy Policy. Ads may redirect you away from our games and our Privacy Policy will not longer apply. We cannot be responsible for the privacy of any information you give outside our games.

Questions

If you have any question or comment about this Privacy Policy, send as an email to hola@donnaipe.com
